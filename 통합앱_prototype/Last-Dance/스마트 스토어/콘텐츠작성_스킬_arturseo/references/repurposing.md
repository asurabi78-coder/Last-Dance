# Content Repurposing Workflows

Strategies for maximizing reach by adapting one piece of content across multiple platforms.

---

## Core Principle

One piece of anchor content should generate 5–10 derivative pieces. Each derivative is **native** to its platform — not a lazy copy-paste. Adjust tone, length, format, and CTA for each destination.

---

## Workflow 1: Blog Post → Multi-Platform

### Source: 1 SEO blog post (1,500–3,000 words)

| Step | Platform | What to Create | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Website | Publish the full blog post | — |
| 2 | LinkedIn | Extract the boldest insight → personal post (150–300 words) | 10 min |
| 3 | X/Twitter | Break into a 7–10 tweet thread | 15 min |
| 4 | Email | Send as a "new post" notification with a unique angle | 10 min |
| 5 | Instagram | Turn key points into a carousel (5–7 slides) | 20 min |
| 6 | YouTube Short | Film a 60-second version of the main takeaway | 15 min |
| 7 | Newsletter | Include as the "big idea" section in your weekly digest | 5 min |

**Total extra time**: ~75 minutes for 6 additional pieces from 1 blog post.

## Workflow 2: YouTube Video → Multi-Platform

### Source: 1 YouTube video (8–15 minutes)

| Step | Platform | What to Create |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Blog | Transcribe + edit into an article (add headers, links, images) |
| 2 | LinkedIn | Pull 3 key lessons → post format |
| 3 | X/Twitter | Thread: "I just published a video on [topic]. Here are the key points:" |
| 4 | Instagram Reels | Cut 3–5 short clips (15–60 seconds) from the best moments |
| 5 | TikTok | Same short clips, adjusted for TikTok trends/music |
| 6 | Email | "New video" email with a teaser insight not in the video |
| 7 | Podcast | Strip the audio → publish as a podcast episode |

---

## Workflow 3: Podcast Episode → Multi-Platform

### Source: 1 podcast episode (30–60 minutes)

| Step | Platform | What to Create |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Blog | Transcribe → edit into a long-form article |
| 2 | YouTube | Publish full video (if recorded) or audiogram |
| 3 | LinkedIn | 5 best quotes → individual posts (schedule over 5 days) |
| 4 | X/Twitter | Thread of the main tips/insights discussed |
| 5 | Instagram | Audiogram clips (15–30 seconds) with captions |
| 6 | Email | Episode summary with one exclusive insight |
| 7 | Newsletter | Curate with guest bio + key takeaways |

## Workflow 4: Case Study → Multi-Platform

### Source: 1 customer case study

| Step | Platform | What to Create |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Website | Full case study page |
| 2 | Blog | Expanded version with industry context |
| 3 | LinkedIn | "Result story" post — before/after with lessons |
| 4 | X/Twitter | Before/after thread with metrics |
| 5 | Email | "Client win" email to nurture sequence |
| 6 | Sales deck | Add 1–2 slides summarizing the case |
| 7 | YouTube | Testimonial video or animated explainer |
| 8 | Landing page | Add metrics + quote to social proof section |

## Workflow 5: Data/Research → Multi-Platform

### Source: Original data, survey, or research report

| Step | Platform | What to Create |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Website | Full report (gated or ungated) |
| 2 | Blog | Key findings article (ungated — SEO bait) |
| 3 | LinkedIn | Infographic or carousel of top stats |
| 4 | X/Twitter | Thread: "We analyzed [N] data points. Here's what we found:" |
| 5 | Email | Exclusive early access or highlight reel |
| 6 | Press | Pitch journalists with the most surprising finding |
| 7 | Webinar | Present the findings live with expert commentary |

---

## Repurposing Rules

1. **Never copy-paste across platforms** — each piece must feel native
2. **Change the angle**: Same topic, different framing per platform
3. **Stagger timing**: Don't publish everywhere simultaneously. Space over 5–7 days.
4. **Link back to the anchor**: Every derivative should drive traffic to the original
5. **Track what works**: Note which derivative formats get the most engagement per platform
6. **Update annually**: Refresh evergreen anchor content and re-run the repurposing cycle

## Content Atomization Framework

For high-value anchor content, atomize into the smallest useful units:

```
Anchor Content (e.g., 3,000-word blog post)
├── Key Insight 1 → LinkedIn post, tweet, email snippet
├── Key Insight 2 → LinkedIn post, tweet, Instagram slide
├── Key Insight 3 → LinkedIn post, tweet, newsletter section
├── Stat/Data Point → Infographic, social image, press pitch
├── Quote/Hot Take → Social post, email subject line
├── How-To Section → YouTube Short, Reel, TikTok
├── FAQ Answer → Featured snippet target, community reply
└── Full Summary → Newsletter, email, podcast topic
```

Each atom is a standalone content piece with its own CTA.
