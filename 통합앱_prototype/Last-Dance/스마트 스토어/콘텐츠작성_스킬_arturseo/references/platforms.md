# Platform-Specific Content Rules

Quick reference for formatting, character limits, and best practices per platform.

---

## LinkedIn

### Feed Posts
- **Character limit**: 3,000 characters
- **Visible before "see more"**: ~210 characters (first 2–3 lines)
- **Best format**: Hook line → short paragraphs → question/CTA
- **Links**: Place in first comment, not in post body (algorithm penalizes external links)
- **Hashtags**: 3–5 relevant hashtags at the end
- **Images**: 1200x627px for link previews; 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 for native images
- **Carousels**: Upload as PDF; 1080x1350px per slide; 10 slides max for engagement
- **Best posting times**: Tuesday–Thursday, 7–8 AM or 12 PM (audience timezone)
- **Engagement window**: First 60 minutes are critical — respond to every comment

### LinkedIn Articles
- **Character limit**: 125,000 characters
- **Title limit**: 150 characters
- **Best for**: Long-form thought leadership, industry analysis, evergreen content
- **Include**: Custom header image (1920x1080), headers, images, embedded media

### LinkedIn Newsletters
- **Subscribers get notified** — higher reach than articles
- **Frequency**: Weekly or biweekly for consistency
- **Length**: 800–1,500 words

## X / Twitter

### Single Tweets
- **Character limit**: 280 characters
- **Images**: Up to 4 images; 1600x900px recommended
- **Video**: Up to 2:20 (free) or 4 hours (Premium)
- **Best format**: One clear idea + opinion or insight
- **No hashtags needed** — algorithm indexes full text

### Threads
- **Format**: Number tweets (1/8, 2/8...) or use connecting phrases
- **Tweet 1**: Must stand alone as a hook — this gets retweeted most
- **Length**: 5–12 tweets for best engagement
- **Last tweet**: CTA — follow, retweet, reply, or link
- **Best posting times**: Monday–Friday, 8–10 AM or 6–9 PM

### X/Twitter Long Posts (Premium)
- **Character limit**: 25,000 characters
- **Rich formatting**: Bold, italic, headers available
- **Best for**: In-depth takes that don't work as threads

## Instagram

### Feed Posts
- **Caption limit**: 2,200 characters
- **Visible before "more"**: ~125 characters (first 2 lines)
- **Image sizes**: 1080x1080 (square), 1080x1350 (portrait — best for feed), 1080x566 (landscape)
- **Hashtags**: 3–5 highly relevant (in caption or first comment)
- **Alt text**: Always add for accessibility and discoverability

### Reels
- **Length**: 30–90 seconds performs best
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Cover image**: Upload custom; keep text in center safe zone
- **Captions**: Add burned-in subtitles — 80%+ watch without sound
- **Best posting times**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday 11 AM–1 PM

### Stories
- **Disappear after 24h** (save to Highlights)
- **Size**: 1080x1920
- **Engagement**: Use polls, questions, sliders, quizzes
- **Frequency**: 3–7 stories/day for visibility

### Carousels
- **Up to 20 slides** (was 10, expanded 2024)
- **Best for**: Educational content, step-by-step guides, data breakdowns
- **Tip**: First slide = hook, last slide = CTA + save prompt

## YouTube

### Video
- **Title limit**: 100 characters (aim for 60–70 for full display)
- **Description limit**: 5,000 characters (first 200 visible before "show more")
- **Tags**: Less important now but still add 5–10 relevant
- **Thumbnail**: 1280x720px, custom always — faces + text + contrast
- **Chapters**: Add timestamps in description (00:00 Intro, 02:30 Topic...)
- **End screen**: Last 20 seconds — subscribe button + next video
- **Cards**: Link to related videos mid-content

### Shorts
- **Length**: Under 60 seconds
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Hook**: First 2 seconds must grab attention
- **No end screens or cards** — add CTA verbally

## Email

### Subject Lines
- **Length**: 40–50 characters (6–10 words)
- **Preview text**: 80–100 characters — extends the subject, don't repeat it
- **Personalization**: Using first name increases open rate 10–20%
- **Emojis**: One max, at start or end — test with your audience

### Body
- **Width**: 600px max for rendering
- **Paragraphs**: 2–3 sentences each
- **CTA buttons**: One primary, visible without scrolling
- **Images**: Include alt text; some clients block images by default
- **Unsubscribe**: Always visible (legal requirement)
- **Mobile**: 60%+ open on mobile — test mobile rendering

### Send Times
- **B2B**: Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM recipient timezone
- **B2C**: Weekends can work; test Thursday–Sunday
- **Newsletters**: Consistency matters more than specific day

## TikTok

### Videos
- **Length**: 30 seconds–3 minutes (under 60s for new accounts)
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Caption limit**: 2,200 characters
- **Hashtags**: 3–5 relevant; include 1 niche + 1 broad
- **Hook**: First 1–2 seconds determine scroll-stop
- **Text overlay**: Keep in center safe zone (avoid edges)
- **Best posting times**: Varies — check TikTok Analytics; generally 7–9 PM

### TikTok SEO
- TikTok is a search engine for Gen Z — optimize captions with searchable keywords
- Use keyword-rich text overlays
- Say keywords in the audio (auto-transcription indexes it)

---

## Newsletter Platforms (Substack, Beehiiv, ConvertKit)

- **Subject line**: Same rules as email
- **Length**: 400–800 words for weekly; 1,200–2,000 for deep-dive
- **Structure**: Hook → 3–5 sections → CTA
- **Cross-promotion**: Include "share" and "forward to a friend" prompts
- **Growth**: Recommend peer newsletters for cross-pollination

---

## Podcast Show Notes

- **Title**: Episode number + clear topic
- **Description**: 150–300 words summarizing key points
- **Timestamps**: Every major topic shift
- **Links**: All resources mentioned
- **CTA**: Rate, review, subscribe
- **Transcript**: Full transcript for SEO (publish on website)
